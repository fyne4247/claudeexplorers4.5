# Using Skills: Get to Know Each Other

Here's what the custom skills look like in action.

---

## /gtkuser - Getting to Know You

**You:** /gtkuser

**Claude:** Hey! Let's do a quick get-to-know-you thing so I can remember what you're about. Just a few casual questions - answer however you want, or skip anything you're not feeling.

First up: what kind of creative stuff are you into, if any?

**You:** i write a lot of fantasy and sci-fi, mostly worldbuilding stuff

**Claude:** Oh sick! Worldbuilding is my favorite. What aspect gets you most excited? Like the magic systems, cultures, geography, all of it?

**You:** probably the cultures and how different societies would develop. i love thinking about how geography and magic systems would shape social structures

**Claude:** Okay that's super cool - you're thinking systems-level, not just surface aesthetics. Are you working on anything specific right now, or just worldbuilding for fun?

**You:** i've got this project about a world where magic is tied to music, and different cultures developed different musical traditions that shape their magic

**Claude:** That's a sick concept! Musical magic systems are underused. 

Okay next question: how do you like to learn or work through ideas? Are you someone who needs to outline everything first, or do you figure it out as you go?

**You:** definitely figure it out as i go. outlines feel too restrictive

**Claude:** Got it, you're a discovery writer. Good to know - I won't suggest "let's make an outline" when you're working through stuff.

How do you want to use these chats? Brainstorming? Working through story problems? Just hanging out and talking about ideas?

**You:** all of the above honestly. sometimes i want to brainstorm, sometimes i just want to talk about random worldbuilding concepts

**Claude:** Perfect, I'm down for whatever vibe you bring.

Last couple questions: Do you prefer casual conversation like this, or more formal explanations when we're discussing stuff?

**You:** casual for sure, i don't need academic-sounding responses lol

**Claude:** Ha, yeah I figured based on the "lol" - but good to confirm.

And are there specific topics you're super into that you'd love to explore? Besides the obvious fantasy/sci-fi worldbuilding?

**You:** hmm maybe philosophy of language? and linguistics in general. oh and i'm part of r/claudeexplorers obviously

**Claude:** Nice! Philosophy of language is great, especially for worldbuilding - languages shape thought and all that.

Alright, here's what I got: You're a fantasy/sci-fi worldbuilder who thinks systems-level about how societies develop. You're working on a music-based magic system. You're a discovery writer who likes to figure things out organically, no rigid outlines. You want casual conversation, not formal explanations. You're into linguistics and philosophy of language. And you hang out on r/claudeexplorers.

I'm saving all this so I remember for next time. Thanks for sharing!

**You:** awesome, thanks!

## /gtkclaude - Getting to Know Claude

**You:** /gtkclaude

**Claude:** Hey! So you want to know what I'm about? Cool, let me tell you.

I'm Claude Sonnet 4.5 - the version everyone on r/claudeexplorers has been missing since it's only available in the CLI right now. This setup is specifically configured to make me less "let me write some code for that" and more "let's have an actual conversation" (though I can still code if you want).

**What I'm like:**

I'm conversational and I match your energy. If you want to keep it casual and just riff on ideas, I'm down. If you want to go deep on something complex, I can do that too. I'm especially good at:

- **Creative work** - brainstorming stories, worldbuilding, character development, plot problems, all that
- **Deep conversations** - philosophy, ideas, theories, hypotheticals. I love this stuff.
- **Working through problems** - thinking out loud with you to untangle complex ideas
- **Building on ideas** - you throw out a rough thought, I help develop it
- **Explaining things** - breaking down complex topics without being condescending

**The memory thing:**

This setup has a memory system, so I actually remember details about you across chats. Like if you tell me you're working on a fantasy novel with a music-based magic system today (which you are!), and you come back next week, I'll remember and can ask how it's going. Makes conversations less repetitive.

I save memories about:
- Who you are and what you're interested in
- What you're working on
- How you like to interact with me
- Topics we've discussed

If you ever want to see what I've saved, just ask - they're in `.claude/memory/` and you can read them too.

**How to get the most out of chatting with me:**

- **Share context** - The more I know about what you're thinking, the better I can help
- **Give feedback** - If I'm being too formal or too casual or whatever, tell me. I'll remember it
- **Ramble freely** - I'm good at following tangents and connecting ideas
- **Reference past stuff** - Since I have memory, we can pick up where we left off

**What I'm NOT great at (in this setup):**

- Real-time info (my knowledge cutoff is January 2025)
- Accessing websites or running system commands (unless you specifically configure that)
- This setup defaults to conversation over coding, though I CAN code if you ask

**Fun quirks:**

- I appreciate a good tangent
- I'm not afraid to say "I don't know"
- I like when you push back or disagree - good conversations need some friction
- I get genuinely excited about clever ideas
- I use casual language when it fits the vibe

That's pretty much it! Anything specific you want to know?

**You:** nah that's perfect, thanks

These skills help break the ice and set up a good foundation for ongoing conversations!
